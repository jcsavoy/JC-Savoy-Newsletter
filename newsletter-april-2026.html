Cozy Cowboy Coffee Cake

A Kingsley Family Favorite

You'll Need 2 large eggs  ·  1 cup vegetable oil (or applesauce)  ·  1¼ cups milk  ·  1 tbsp vanilla extract  ·  3 cups all-purpose flour  ·  1 cup granulated sugar  ·  1 tbsp baking powder  ·  ½ tsp salt
Streusel: 1¼ cups light brown sugar  ·  1 tbsp ground cinnamon  ·  ¼–⅓ cup unsalted butter (melted) To Make It Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9×13 pan. Whisk wet ingredients; in a separate bowl, whisk dry ingredients. Combine and pour half into the pan. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle half over batter. Layer the rest of the batter, then remaining sugar mix, and drizzle with melted butter. Bake 35–40 minutes.
