Bette's "Impending Disaster" Cinnamon Scones

As featured in *The List*

You'll Need 3 cups all-purpose flour  ·  1/3 cup sugar  ·  1 tbsp baking powder  ·  1/2 tsp salt  ·  3/4 cup cold butter (cubed)  ·  1 cup heavy cream  ·  1 large egg  ·  2 tsp vanilla
Cinnamon Swirl: 1/4 cup sugar  ·  1 tsp cinnamon  ·  1 tbsp melted butter To Make It Whisk dry ingredients. Cut in butter until it looks like crumbs. Whisk cream, egg, and vanilla; stir into dry until just combined. Fold in your cinnamon swirl. Pat into a 1-inch thick circle, cut into 8 wedges. Bake at 400°F for 18–22 minutes. Best served warm while contemplating your life choices.

"I don't care if you're on a diet," Bette says. "These scones have a purpose. Usually, that purpose is making you forget that you’re about to spend four hours cleaning a park with your ex."
